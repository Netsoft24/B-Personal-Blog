About

The Slow Dispatch is a personal blog about design, simplicity, and the quiet art of doing fewer things with greater intention.

B

Brandon Ellis

Portland, Oregon

I'm a designer and writer who believes the best work comes from restraint, not excess. After years of chasing productivity hacks and optimizing every minute, I found more meaning in slowing down. This blog is where I explore that shift — through essays on mindful design, intentional living, and the code we write along the way.

What This Blog Is About

Simplicity

Stripping away the unnecessary to reveal what actually matters. In design, in code, and in the way we spend our days.

Intentional Design

Every choice should earn its place. Good design isn't about adding more — it's about making deliberate decisions that serve people.

Slow Living

Resisting the culture of speed. Finding beauty in patience, in empty space, and in the things that take time to unfold.

Stay in the loop

New essays land every other week. No spam, no noise — just thoughtful writing delivered to your inbox.
